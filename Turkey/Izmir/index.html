Five Famous Places in Izmir

İzmir Historical Elevator Building

Charming historical tower offering sweeping ocean views, a cafe & a viewing platform.

MaviBahçe

Lively shopping center with local brands, a food court, casual restaurants & an IMAX cinema.

Izmir Wildlife Park

Large zoo with African, Asian, European & American animals, kids' play areas & dining.

Izmir Clocktower

This elegant monument which was built in 1901, decorates Konak Square and has become the symbol of the city of Izmir.

Hisar Mosque

Landmark 1590s mosque featuring a domed interior with a mix of Ottoman & European-style art.
