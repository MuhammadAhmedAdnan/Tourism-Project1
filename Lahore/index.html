Five Famous Places in Lahore

Badshahi Mosque

One of the largest mosques in the world, known for its grandeur.

Lahore Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site, showcasing Mughal architecture.

Shalimar Gardens

Beautiful gardens built by the Mughals, famous for its terraces.

Wazir Khan Mosque

Known for its intricate tile work and vibrant frescoes.

Food Street

Experience Lahore's culinary delights in the heart of the old city.
