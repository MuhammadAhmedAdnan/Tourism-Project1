Five Famous Places in Ankara

Ankara Castle

Ankara Castle (Turkish: Ankara Kalesi) is a historic fortification in the city of Ankara, Turkey, constructed in or after the 7th century.

Gençlik Parkı

Gençlik Parkı (literally Youth Park) is a public park in Ankara, Turkey .

Şehit Cuma Dağ Tabiat Tarihi Müzesi

A must see place in Ankara. Great place for all the age groups. You can spend a quality couple of hours

Eymir Lake

Narrow, curving lake surrounded by pine trees on the Middle East Technical University campus.

Kocatepe Mosque

Built 1967–1987, this neoclassical Ottoman-style mosque is the city's largest, accommodating 24,000.
